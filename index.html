Who We Are

Building the future,
together.

ISU Blockchain & Finance Club is a student community at Istinye University dedicated to exploring blockchain technology, decentralized finance (DeFi), AI-powered financial solutions, digital assets, and the Web3 ecosystem through research, events, and collaboration.
